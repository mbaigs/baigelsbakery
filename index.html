Fudgy Brownies

Prep: 15 min • Bake: 25 min • Yield: 16 squares

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (113g) butter
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (60g) cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) flour
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F / 175°C.
  2. Melt butter, whisk in sugar, then eggs.
  3. Stir in cocoa, flour, and salt.
  4. Bake 22–28 minutes. Cool, slice, enjoy.
