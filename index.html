Bakery Style Cookies

Prep: 15 min • Bake: 11-13 min • Yield: no clue

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup (180g) flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 stick of butter (113g) room temp
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp (130g) lightly packed light brown sugar
  • 1 egg room temp
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (I like to cut up 70% lindt bars)
  • optional 1/2 tsp cornstarch per cup of flour for chewier thicker cookies

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cornstarch if using
  2. Beat the butter with both sugars until creamy (around 2 minutes) with electric mixer
  3. Add in vanilla and eggs and beat until just combined
  4. Stir in flour mixture and chocolate chips
  5. Wrap dough in plastic rap and refrigerate for 24-72 hours to let flavors develop
  6. Preheat oven to 350°F / 175°C.
  7. Place cookie douch lumps on parchment paper (do not grease)and bake for 11-13 min
  8. Optional: Cover cookues with some flaky sea salt before baking
